About wurstbot.com

I post here IT various snippets. You can find out a bit about me.

Originally wurstbot.com was built as an Django cms-like application. While Django is awesome and all, currently, the site is static HTML compiled from markdown.

Why? It's easier to operate and requires smaller footprint setup.
Load times are lower and the site is more secure.

I used stackedit to make the site. It's simple to use, has a nice preview and supports a wide range of markdown extras.

Stackedit comes with cool icons. A full list of these icons including preview can be found here: fontello demo
